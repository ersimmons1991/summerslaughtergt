Age of Sigmar Grand Tournament

Our Age of Sigmar GT is a 5 round, 2 day ITC tournament played at 2,000 points and following the Pitched Battle/Matched Play rules. Maximum Player Occupancy for this event is capped at 60. Official Games Workshop Battle Tomes, Warscrolls, and supplements published on or prior to June 27th, 2020, will be permitted. Forge World Armies (Legions of Azgorh), Units, and other supplements/rules will be permitted for this event. Malign Sorcery, GW Hidden Agendas, and Mysterious Terrain will be used. Realm Spells, Realm Features, and Realm Commands will be used at this event. See the rules primer for more details once published.

Note

Since the General's Handbook 2020 will be releasing in June, we'll be using the new missions from that book. In the mean time, the current GHB2019 missions referred will be good for practice.
