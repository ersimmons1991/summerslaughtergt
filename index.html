Format

This event will be 1500 points with a maximum of three detachments using the Warhammer 40k 8th edition Matched Play rules. Official Games Workshop codexes and supplements published on or prior to July 7th will be permitted. All Beta Matched Play rules and guidelines will be in effect. Forge World units and rules will not be permitted for this event.

Armies should consist of WYSIWYG models, but conversions and rule-of-cool counts-as may be permitted at the TO’s discretion. Armies must be painted in good faith to at least a 3 color standard. White primer and a dusting of 2 colors of spray is not acceptable! If you have any questions about your army or conversions, please send an email to sam@samlucidi.com with the words "Summer Slaughter" in the subject line.

Summer Slaughter is an ITC event.
