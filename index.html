About

The Basement War Gamers are proud to present Summer Slaughter 2021, a Warhammer 40,000 and Age of Sigmar Grand Tournament. Event to take place at St. Gabe's Lodge located at 525 Jefferson St, Stowe, PA 19464. Space limited to 50 Warhammer 40k players and 40 Age of Sigmar players. Event is first come first served. Tickets are purchasable through Best Coast Pairings at the links below.

Each event ticket comes with hot buffet lunch on Saturday and cold buffet lunch on Sunday. Yuengling Lager draft beer and Coors Light draft beer also included in the ticket price for those attendees that are 21 years of age or older with valid proof of age. Water, soda, and mixed drinks extra at the bar areas.

Face Masks and/or Face Shields will be required to participate. A temperature check will take place each day for all attendees for the safety of all venue personnel, event staff, and participants.

Events

Warhammer 40k Grand Tournament

Our Warhammer 40k event is a 5 round, 2 day Chapter Approved tournament played at 2,000 points. Official Games Workshop codexes and supplements published on or prior to July 31st will be permitted. The maximum number of players for this event is capped at 50.

Age of Sigmar Grand Tournament

Our Age of Sigmar GT is a 5 round, 2 day ITC tournament played at 2,000 points and following the Pitched Battle/Matched Play rules. The maximum number of players for this event is capped at 40.

Official Games Workshop Battle Tomes, Warscrolls, and supplements published on or prior to July 31st will be permitted. Forge World Armies (Legions of Azgorh), Units, and other supplements/rules will be permitted for this event as long as they are current in the GHB 2020. Endless Spells from Malign Sorcery, GW Auxikiary Objectives, and Mysterious Terrain will be used. Realm Spells, Realm Features, and Realm Commands will be used at this event. Should Games Workshop release Age of Sigmar 3.0 and/or General's Handbook 2021 on or before July 17th, the AoS event staff will update the event packet accordingly. Full Event packet to follow.
